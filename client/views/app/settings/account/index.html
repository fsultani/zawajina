Account Information

Deactivate account

You may deactivate your account to hide your profile from search results.

Your information will remain in our database if you decide to reactivate it in the future.

To reactivate your account, simply log in.

Delete Account

You may permanently delete your account.

Your information will be removed from our database.

This option cannot be reverted: once you delete your account, you will not be able to reactivate it.
